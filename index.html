About

I’m a developer who builds with intention. I care deeply about how things feel, not just whether they technically work. Timing, spacing, transitions, performance — the details most people overlook are the ones I obsess over.


It all started with wanting to create something that didn’t already exist — or to improve something until it felt right. Over time, that mindset turned into a focus on systems, design, and development that scale without losing quality.

What I Do

I work primarily in web development and interactive systems, while constantly experimenting with software, tooling, and creative technology. I enjoy building publicly finished work, while privately iterating on ideas until they’re ready.


HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Java, Lua · Blender · Roblox Studio · GitHub Pages · Vercel

Scale & Impact

I’ve assisted in the development of two major Roblox games and currently contribute to large-scale systems with tens of thousands of daily users. Working under real performance limits — older hardware, massive player counts, and live updates — has shaped how I build.


I’ve learned that slowing down and refining almost always beats rushing something out — even when a lot of people are waiting. Quality compounds.

Philosophy

To me, quality isn’t about excess detail — it’s about care. A smooth animation instead of a hard cut. A system that degrades gracefully instead of failing loudly. Thoughtful design that respects both performance and people.


I look up to builders who combine technical depth with clarity and creativity — people who can scale systems without sacrificing soul.
